Links
Links to related projects:
  • CMU Flite: small footprint speech synthesizer.
  • Festival at Edinburgh: home page of CSTR's Festival Speech Synthesis System.
  • Edinburgh Speech Tools: home page of CSTR's Speech Tools Library.
  • MBROLA: the MBROLA project based at Faculté Polytechnique de Mons. This project offers diphone synthesizer for many languages, which can be used as the basis for new full TTS voices. (local US mirror is available here)
  • OGI TTS Group Oregon Graduate Institute offer new voices in Festival (English, Spanish and German).
  • HMM-based Speech Synthesis System Nagoya Institute of Technologies, toolkit for building HMNM-based voices.
  • IMS, University of Stuttgart, TTS group offer a text pre-processor for German for Festival, and have a full on-line German TTS example.
  • The EMU Speech Database System offers speech display and label editing software useful in building, and diagnosis faults in synthetic voices. (local US mirror is available here)
  • CMU Speech Software Resources, a collection of synthesis, recognition and dialog free software distributed by CMU.
